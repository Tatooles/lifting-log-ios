Support

Help for account access, sync, exports, local data, and account deletion in Lifting Log.

Contact

Email support@liftinglog.app for support.

We read every email. Include your device model, iOS version, what you were doing, and whether you were signed in when the issue happened.

Signing in and syncing

  • You can log workouts locally without signing in.
  • Signing in enables account-backed sync through Lifting Log's production backend.
  • If sync appears stuck, check your network connection, reopen the app, and confirm you are still signed in.

Exporting your data

Workout history export is available from Settings. Export before deleting data if you want a personal copy of your workout history.

Local data and offline use

Lifting Log works without an account. If you stay signed out, workout history and settings remain local to your device unless you export or delete them from Settings.

Deleting your account

Signed-in users can delete their account in the app from Settings under Privacy & Data. Signed-out users can delete local data from the same area.

Export first if you want to keep a personal copy of your workout history.

Privacy Policy

Read the Lifting Log Privacy Policy for details about local data, account data, sync, export, and deletion.
